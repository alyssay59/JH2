Aboout Us! The world behind a small buisness

The Owner
Heather Newman is the woman who started it all. She is the epitome of determination and working for what she has. Along with owning and creating products for JH2, she is a mother of three, a sister, a daughter, and a registered travel nurse. Even with working a full time nursing job and supporting her children, she still managed to start up JH2.
The Story Behind The Name
The name "JH2" comes from two of Heather's best friends, Jay and Julie, who tragically passed away in a car accident a few years ago. The JH2 come from the initials of Jay and Julie Hogan. In an attempt to keep their memory alive, Heather didn't only sell custom spartan head JH2 monograms to raise money she also decided to dedicate the name of her buisness to her two closest friends.
The Start Up
JH2 started from Heather just doing monograms to get extra cash. She would cut out monograms and then post pictures on her facebook account where she sold most of the product. Slowly she began to add to the products. The inventory raised from heat transfer vinyl to all vinyl and not only initials but soon enough all kinds of designs. She then started buying yetis in bulk and letting people design them how they wanted. With this design, she would traansfer the design to the cup and get it to them as soon as possible.
Our mission
Our mission is not to make millions. We strive to make people happy. We want our customers to be able to express themselves through their own ideas and designs. This is the reason that we keep our products customizable.We don't plan on expanding the buisness at the moment. As of right now the store is only online. The reason for this is because we want to keep the buisness small so that we can ensure that each item is homemade with love.
